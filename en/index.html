The Challenge

Japan is a unique market.
Here's why it's hard to crack alone.

Japan is the world's third-largest economy — but entering it without local expertise is costly and slow.

🗾

Platforms are different

Yahoo! Japan holds significant ad market share alongside Google. Meta works differently here. Knowing which platform fits your product matters.

🈳

Language isn't just translation

Japanese consumers respond to tone, nuance, and context. A word-for-word translation rarely converts. You need copy and messaging that feels native.

🔗

The funnel is different

LINE dominates CRM. Trust is built slowly. The customer journey in Japan requires a different approach to nurturing and conversion.
